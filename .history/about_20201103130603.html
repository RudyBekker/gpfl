    About Us 
    Your trusted friend in Logistics

    Golden Pearl Freight Logistics cc is a transport company established in 2009 and the BBBEE status of the company is Level 1.

    The primary purpose of the business is the transportation of goods/items from various companies. We are currently operating out of Verulam; Durban and we aim to provide the following business objectives.

    • Provide an efficient transportation solution for our customers.
    • Expand our current fleet to accommodate the growing needs of the business .
    • Provide a safe working environment for staff
    • Promote business success through dedication, service and integrity .
    This dynamic team brings a wealth of experience from a business and professional perspective. The focus of the team is to provide excellent service to our customers all the time.


    Charity Initiatives

    Golden Pearl Freight Solutions also support various charity organisations as we see the value in the community and helping others.
